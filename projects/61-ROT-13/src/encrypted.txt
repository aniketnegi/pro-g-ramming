VS 
  (Ehqlneq Xvcyvat)

-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-

Vs lbh pna xrrc lbhe urnq jura nyy nobhg
  Ner ybfvat gurvef naq oynzvat vg ba lbh,
Vs lbh pna gehfg lbhefrys jura nyy zra qbhog lbh,
  Ohg znxr nyybjnapr sbe gurve qbhogvat gbb;
Vs lbh pna jnvg naq abg or gverq ol jnvgvat,
  Be orvat yvrq nobhg, qba’g qrny va yvrf,
Be orvat ungrq, qba’g tvir jnl gb ungvat,
  Naq lrg qba’g ybbx gbb tbbq, abe gnyx gbb jvfr:

Vs lbh pna qernz—naq abg znxr qernzf lbhe znfgre;
  Vs lbh pna guvax—naq abg znxr gubhtugf lbhe nvz;
Vs lbh pna zrrg jvgu Gevhzcu naq Qvfnfgre
  Naq gerng gubfr gjb vzcbfgbef whfg gur fnzr;
Vs lbh pna orne gb urne gur gehgu lbh’ir fcbxra
  Gjvfgrq ol xanirf gb znxr n genc sbe sbbyf,
Be jngpu gur guvatf lbh tnir lbhe yvsr gb, oebxra,
  Naq fgbbc naq ohvyq ’rz hc jvgu jbea-bhg gbbyf:

Vs lbh pna znxr bar urnc bs nyy lbhe jvaavatf
  Naq evfx vg ba bar ghea bs cvgpu-naq-gbff,
Naq ybfr, naq fgneg ntnva ng lbhe ortvaavatf
  Naq arire oerngur n jbeq nobhg lbhe ybff;
Vs lbh pna sbepr lbhe urneg naq areir naq fvarj
  Gb freir lbhe ghea ybat nsgre gurl ner tbar,
Naq fb ubyq ba jura gurer vf abguvat va lbh
  Rkprcg gur Jvyy juvpu fnlf gb gurz: ‘Ubyq ba!’

Vs lbh pna gnyx jvgu pebjqf naq xrrc lbhe iveghr,
  Be jnyx jvgu Xvatf—abe ybfr gur pbzzba gbhpu,
Vs arvgure sbrf abe ybivat sevraqf pna uheg lbh,
  Vs nyy zra pbhag jvgu lbh, ohg abar gbb zhpu;
Vs lbh pna svyy gur hasbetvivat zvahgr
  Jvgu fvkgl frpbaqf’ jbegu bs qvfgnapr eha,
Lbhef vf gur Rnegu naq rirelguvat gung’f va vg,
  Naq—juvpu vf zber—lbh’yy or n Zna, zl fba!
